Pook's Pick


Hit or Shit

Dirty Grandpaw

“Didn’t this come out like two years ago?”
-America upon seeing the trailer for “Dirty Grandpa”, 2015


Just so you know that was “Bad Grandpa” you idiot, who can get one word confused?
So is this a sequel? A prequel to a Johnny Knoxville movie? What exactly is “Dirty Grandpa”?


Well let me tell you: it’s a pretty funny comedy

“Dirty Grandpa” is terrible in almost every way, the story is god awful, the script is a disaster and the
direction is uninspired, so why is it a hit? Get a few of your close bros together and give it a watch, it’s a good damn time.

It’s really hard for me to review comedies. Did it make me laugh? Yes. Was it profound? Well fuck no. The movie is nothing but sex jokes and bro humor but I’m not gonna be pretentious, if it makes me giggle then it is a GOOD COMEDY. I feel like comedies and horrors are on different scales from other genres, as they are mostly hindered by their genre and it’s hard to overcome it.

The humor here is nothing classy as previously mentioned. The acting is as good as it needs to be, Efron is quickly becoming a dude-bro comedy powerhouse. Deniro looks like he is having fun. Plaza is the surprise to me, she seems far above this kind of thing and really seems out of place.

What else can I say? Terrible movie, good comedy. It set out to be fucking stupid and boy did it do it.

Highlights: Not serious at all, good to watch with friends, some seriously funny bits, Efron and Deniro are having fun, Plaza’s ass
Lowlights: A lot of jokes do miss, no story to speak of, no heart, periods of no laughter
